Welcome to the AI Card Game

In this game, you will explore various applications of facial recognition technology and its level of acceptance. Your task is to arrange the cards in the "Card Placement" area based on their levels of acceptance, from most acceptable to least acceptable.

The game includes a set of cards, each representing a specific application of facial recognition technology. Your goal is to identify card combos that are believed to have similar levels of acceptance. Card combos consist of three cards placed together that reflect similar perspectives on AI acceptance.

Drag and drop the cards into the "Card Placement" area in the order you think represents their level of acceptance, with the most acceptable at the top and the least acceptable at the bottom. As you place the cards, pay attention to the combos you can create, as they will earn you bonus points!

Score points by placing cards correctly and achieving card combos. You will earn 10 points for each correctly placed card and an additional 50 points for each achieved card combo. Aim for the highest score possible!

Are you ready? Start arranging the cards and explore the different levels of acceptance of facial recognition technology!
